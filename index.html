A Societal Mission

The societal mission of Air University is to provide comprehensive education, training,
and leadership development for military and civilian personnel within the United States Air Force.
Through undergraduate and graduate programs, professional military education, and research initiatives,
Air University aims to cultivate the skills and knowledge necessary for effective leadership in air and space operations.
The institution is dedicated to advancing the understanding and application of air and space power,contributing to the
development of innovative strategies and technologies that enhance the capabilities of the U.S.
